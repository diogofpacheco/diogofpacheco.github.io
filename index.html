Welcome / Bem-Vindo.

My name is Diogo F. Pacheco. I am a PhD Canditate under the supervision of Prof. Ronaldo Menezes at Florida Institute of Technology. My research interests lie at the exploration of social media data via the examination of the digital footprints as a proxy for understanding human behavior. I am member of BioComplex Lab and I also colaborate as an invited researcher of Computational Intelligence Research Group at University of Pernambuco. Here, you can find my publications and curriculum vitae.
